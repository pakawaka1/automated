About ABC

Since 1991, Automated Building Components, Inc. (ABC), has designing, engineering, and manufacturing wood roof trusses.

The company was founded by David Cervantes, his wife Violet, and his brother Gabriel.

Before opening ABC, David has worked for a few truss companies in the area. With the experience he developed over the years from nearly aspect of the roof truss manufacturing process, including working modular saw, on the tables, and in the office, David gained the knowledge necessary to start his own business.

ABC has been located at their current location on 2853 South Orange Avenue since 1995. They continue to built well-built, reasonably priced wood roof trusses.
